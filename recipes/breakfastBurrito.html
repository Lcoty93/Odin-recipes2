Breakfast Burrito

I eat this every morning for on the go.

Macros: 37g Protein, 38g Carbs, 15.5g Fat, per serving.

Makes 5 servings.

Ingredients

  • Egg Whites
  • Sausage
  • Reduced Fat Fiesta Blend Cheese
  • Tortilla
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Cook Egg Whites.
  2. Add Cheese to Egg Whites.
  3. Cook Sausage.
  4. Combine cooked Sausage, Egg, and Cheese into a Burrito.
  5. Roll it all up.
  6. Cook on both sides evenly.
