High Protein Chili

Very high protein Chili, great for cold days.

Macros: 19g Protein, 28g Carbs, 5g Fat, per serving.

Makes 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean Ground Turkey
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 1 Orange Bell Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Garlic
  • 1 can of no salt added Corn
  • 1 can of no salt added Black Beans
  • 1 can Chili Beans
  • 2 cups Tomatoes, crushed, canned
  • 1/4 cup Chili Powder
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop 1/2 Red Onion and Bell Pepper and set aside.
  2. Pre-heat pan, add Olive Oil, and Garlic, Red Onion, and Bell Pepper. Season with salt & Pepper. Cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Add ground Turkey. Season with Salt & Pepper.
  4. Pull out Crock Pot.
  5. Empty Water, Corn, Black beans (drained), Chili beans, Tomatoes, into Crock Pot.
  6. Once ground Turkey is finished cooking, add to crockpot.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and chili powder.
  8. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or High on 3-4 hours.
