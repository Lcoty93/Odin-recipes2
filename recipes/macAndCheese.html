Healthy Mac and Cheese

This is one of my go to healthy Mac and Cheese recipes.

Macros: 26g Protein, 53g Carbs, 5g Fat, per serving.

Makes 2 servings.

Ingredients

  • Fat Free Cottage Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese Powder (Hoosier Hill Farm)
  • Fat Free Milk
  • Protein Pasta

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 servings of protein pasta. (4oz)
  2. Drain pasta and put back into pot.
  3. 2 servings of Cottage Cheese. (226g)
  4. 4 servings of Cheddar Cheese Powder. (32g)
  5. 1/2 cup Fat Free Milk.
  6. Blend together in a blender.
  7. Add to Protein Pasta and mix.
